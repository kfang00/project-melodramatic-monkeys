Places I've Visited

Chicago

I went to Chicago with my family. We went during a heat wave so I spent most of the trip trying not to melt. I remember visiting the Chicago Bean, but the rest of the trip was a hot blur.

Bahamas

The Bahamas is such a beautiful place, and I'm so happy I got the chance to go. I have a lot of fond memories of this trip. The highlight of it was probably when I got to kiss a dolphin!

Hong Kong

I went to Hong Kong when I was very young. Originally, I was actually going to live there permanently because my dad moved there for work. I ended up only staying for the summer, but I had a lot of fun there!

Places I Want To Visit

I want to travel to Italy, Korea, France, Japan, and many other places. It has been such a long time since I last travelled so any place sounds exciting!
